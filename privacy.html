Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect personal information submitted through this website.

Last updated: April 2026. This policy may be updated as XPharma's official operations and applicable privacy requirements evolve.

1. Who we are

XPharma is responsible for personal information collected through this institutional website. We do not sell products directly through this channel; the site is intended for information and contact requests.

2. Information we collect

We collect only information voluntarily submitted through the contact form:

  • Full name
  • Company or clinic name (optional)
  • Email address
  • Phone / WhatsApp number (optional)
  • Message content

We do not intentionally collect additional personal information beyond what is necessary for the technical operation of the site.

3. How we use information

Information submitted through the form is used only to:

  • Respond to your message or contact request
  • Route your inquiry to the appropriate internal team, such as product information, commercial support, or export review

We do not use form submissions for automated advertising or sell personal information to third parties.

4. Cookies and similar technologies

This site may use essential technical cookies or browser storage required for basic functionality. We do not currently use third-party advertising or analytics cookies.

If this changes, this policy should be updated before implementation.

5. Information sharing

XPharma does not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties for commercial advertising purposes. Your information may be accessed by internal personnel who need it to respond to your request.

6. Your rights

Depending on your location and applicable privacy laws, you may have rights to:

  • Confirm whether your personal information is being processed
  • Request access, correction, or deletion of your information
  • Withdraw consent when processing is based on consent

To make a privacy request, contact us through the contact form.

7. Policy updates

This policy reflects the current state of this institutional website and may be updated when there are meaningful changes in how personal information is handled. The last updated date appears at the top of this page.

Questions about privacy or data protection? Contact us through the form.
