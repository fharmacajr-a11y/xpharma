FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Does XPharma provide manufacturing documentation? +

XPharma communicates manufacturing documentation and quality information through official channels. Product-specific documentation may vary by product and market and is available upon request.

How can I become an XPharma distributor? +

Submit the form above and select "Become a distributor." Our commercial team will review your profile, target market, and next steps for authorized distribution discussions.

Where can I find product line information? +

Visit the Products page and the LipoRED HD page for current product presentations. For specific details, contact the XPharma team through official channels.

Does XPharma support international commercial inquiries? +

Yes. For export or international distribution questions, select "Partnership proposal" in the form and include the destination market, company profile, and expected volume.

Is product information support available? +

Yes. XPharma can provide commercial and product information guidance for professional, distributor, and institutional inquiries. This site does not provide professional health guidance or individualized product-use guidance.
